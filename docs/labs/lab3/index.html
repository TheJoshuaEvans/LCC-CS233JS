ITS FARKLE

Number of Players

Game Rules:

Each player takes turns rolling dice, attempting to roll scoring combos
A player starts their turn rolling 6 dice
If a player rolls and cannot score at any time, they lose their turn
If a player rolls and does score they may roll the dice that remain
If a player scores and no dice remain, they may roll 6 dice to continue
A player wins once they have accumulated 10,000 points

Scoring Rules:

1 = 100 points
5 = 50 points
1, 1, 1 = 1,000 points
#, #, # = # x 100
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 = 3,000 points
3 pairs = 1,500 points
Highest scoring combo will be exclusively counted in case of overlap
For example: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 will only score 3000 points
